PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Many individuals especially students and teenagers experience stress,mood changes, loneliness and emotional pressure in their day to day life. However, not everyone has someone available to talk to , express their feelings or recieve some instant emotional support.
There is a need for a friendly, and accessible companion that can talk, respond to emotions, track moods, and provide supportive suggestions like music, affirmations or break reminders.

The aim of this project is to solve that gap by developing a SOCIAL COMPANION BOT that interacts with the user, logs their moods, analyses their behavioral patterns, and provides emotional support in real-time.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT: 

The scope of this project includes:

Creating an interactive-line based social companion bot

Implementing response based on the keywords used.

It allows the users to chat with the bot like a supportive friend.

Storing and tracking the mood logs to identify behavioral patterns.

Providing recommendations such as music playlists, affirmations, and break suggestions.

Displaying behaviour summaries based on stored mood data.

Keeping the system simple so that it can run without the use of any APIs or complex machine learning.

The project focuses on the emotional support, and is meant for general well-being assistance

TARGET USERS:

This project is made for:

Students dealing with stress or who require motivation..

People who are looking for a non-judging companion to express their emotions.

Users who are looking for a mood tracking tool.

Anyone who needs a quick, supportive, everyday companion.

HIGH-LEVEL FEATURES:

The Social Companion Bot offers the following major features:

1. Chat Module:

It responds to greetings, emotions, stress, tiredness, sadness, happiness, questions, and general conversation.

Provides empathetic and supportive responses.

2. Mood & Stress Logging:

Users have the privilige to to add their emotional logs manually.

In a single session multiple number of logs can be stored.

Logs are further stored in a textfile.

3. Suggestion System

Suggests music playlists based on the user mood.

Gives positive afirmations based on the emotional state.

Offers basic break recommendations.

4. Behaviour summary:

Bot analyses the last 5 patterns entered by the user.

It detects patterns such as sadness, stress, happiness

Gives feedback on emotional trends.

5. Menu-based Navigation:

Clean menu with 5 simple options:

Chat

Log mood

Suggestions (music + break + affirmations)

Behaviour Summary

Exit

6. Persistent Mood Storage

All moods appended to companion.txt.

Used for tracking and pattern recognition.
